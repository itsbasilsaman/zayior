FAQs

Have questions?

Find answers to common questions about your stay at Zayior Resorts, the best resort in Kakkadampoyil, nestled in 32 hectares of lush forest with stunning lakeside views.

What are the check-in and check-out times at Zayior Resorts?

Our standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability at our Kakkadampoyil resort.

Is Zayior Resorts family-friendly?

Absolutely! As one of the best resorts near Kozhikode for families, we offer spacious accommodations and activities suitable for all ages. Our 32-hectare property with lakes and forests provides plenty of space for children to explore safely.

What makes Zayior Resorts the best in Kakkadampoyil?

Zayior Resorts stands out with its breathtaking 360-degree views, 32 hectares of pristine forest, two serene lakes, and exceptional service. Our unique location offers unmatched tranquility while being conveniently accessible from Calicut, making us the best resort in Kakkadampoyil.

What activities are available at the resort?

Guests can enjoy nature walks through our lush forests, lakeside relaxation, bird watching, and photography of the stunning 360-degree views. We also arrange local sightseeing tours to explore the beautiful surroundings of Kakkadampoyil and nearby Calicut.
