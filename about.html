About us

Experience the Best Resort in Kakkadampoyil

Welcome to Zayior Resorts, the ultimate destination for those seeking tranquility and adventure in the heart of Kakkadampoyil. Spread across 32 hectares of lush greenery, our resort is embraced by dense forests on three sides, with two serene lakes adding to its charm and offering stunning 360-degree panoramic views. Discover a haven of peace and natural beauty — perfect for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.

Lake Access

Enjoy direct access to our pristine lake, where you can fish, kayak, or simply soak in the tranquil waterside atmosphere and watch the local wildlife.

Swimming Pool

Dive into relaxation with our sparkling swimming pool, perfect for cooling off on warm days or enjoying a refreshing morning swim surrounded by nature.

Trekking by walk

Explore scenic trails through lush landscapes with our guided trekking experiences, suitable for all skill levels and offering breathtaking views.

360° Viewpoint

Experience panoramic vistas from our exclusive viewpoint, offering 360-degree views of the surrounding mountains, valleys, and breathtaking sunsets.

Play Area

A safe and exciting space for kids to enjoy slides, swings, and outdoor games—perfect for family fun under the open sky.

Campfire

Gather around the warmth of the campfire, share stories, roast marshmallows, and enjoy magical nights under the stars.
