About the location

A peaceful escape in the heart of Kakkadampoyil

Nestled in the scenic Western Ghats of Kerala, Zayior Resorts is located in the tranquil village of Kakkadampoyil — just a short drive from Kozhikode. Surrounded by dense forests, waterfalls, and misty hills, this location offers a blend of untouched nature and refreshing mountain air, making it a perfect retreat for families and nature lovers seeking peace and rejuvenation.
